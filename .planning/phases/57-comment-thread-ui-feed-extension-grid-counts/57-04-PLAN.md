## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-57-10 | Information Disclosure | gated thread/count leak | mitigate | CommentThread renders [] for gated viewers (getCommentsForTarget gate) and CommentList shows CommentGateLocked instead of the list; no count is derived for gated viewers (D-04) |
