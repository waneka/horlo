<objective>
Insert a prominent, friction-to-skip "Add your photos" step into the `AddWatchFlow` state machine (PHOTO-09), placed after the watch is created and before final navigation.

Purpose: SC5 requires the add-watch flow to surface photo upload as a first-class, not-easily-skipped step. The architecturally tricky part is the watchId timing: photos attach to an existing watch row, so the flow must capture the watchId after `addWatch` succeeds but before navigating away. This plan threads that via an optional `onWatchCreated` callback on `WatchForm` and a new `photos-pending` FlowState, then renders a leaner `WatchPhotoStep` (drop zone + per-file progress + CTAs) that reuses the upload mechanics.
Output: `flowTypes.ts` + `WatchForm.tsx` + `AddWatchFlow.tsx` modified; new `WatchPhotoStep.tsx`; the add-watch-flow test populated.
</objective>
