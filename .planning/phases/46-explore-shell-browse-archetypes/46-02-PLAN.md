run query-free when a facet is present, surface each facet as a removable
inline chip, and render an archetype editorial header (D-09..D-14).

functional. It is the most cross-cutting change in the phase — a mechanical
layer-by-layer extension of the Phase 40 movement/size/style facet pattern.

without a typed query when any facet is active.
