## Decisions Made

- **archetype-echo template id preserved.** The narrative pivots to era ("Another ${era} piece — leaning further into the era you favor") but the template's `id` remains `'archetype-echo'` — this avoids silently breaking any consumer that keys off id (caches, persisted state). The id is a stable identifier; the semantics are what changed.
- **first-watch predicate pivot is a Rule 3 fix, not part of D-VERDICT-01.** The original `profile.dominantArchetype !== null` referenced a field that D-VERDICT-03 removed. The substituted `profile.dominantEraSignal !== null` preserves the semantic intent (empty profile detection) via the surviving sibling. Documented inline in the predicate comment.
- **Scenario 5 of fit-delta.test.ts deleted outright (not rewritten).** The test's ground truth — `'Different archetype: Dive vs Dress'` — no longer exists because the archetype dimension is gone from the ranked output. Era-axis coverage is already preserved by Scenario 6.
- **CatalogEntry / CatalogTasteAttributes `primaryArchetype: null` fields left on 5 test fixtures.** The fields are still type-required (Plan 06 owns the type-level removal); setting `null` is the minimal-shape-parity choice. When Plan 06 lands, those 5 lines + composer.ts:107 become deletable in a single sweep.
